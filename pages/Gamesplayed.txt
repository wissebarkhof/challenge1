'''games played''' (most often abbreviated as '''g''' or '''gp''') is a [[statistic]] used in [[team sport]]s to indicate the total number of games in which a player has participated (in any capacity); the statistic is generally applied irrespective of whatever portion of the game is contested.  ==baseball== in [[baseball]], the [[baseball statistics|statistic]] applies also to players who, prior to a game, are included on a [[starting lineup]] card or are announced as ''ex ante'' substitutes, whether or not they play,<ref>[http://mlb.mlb.com/mlb/official_info/official_rules/official_scorer_10.jsp section 20 of the official scorer guidelines]</ref> although, in [[major league baseball]], the application of this statistic does not extend to consecutive games played streaks. a [[starting pitcher]], then, may be credited with a game played even as he is not credited with a [[games started|game started]] or an [[innings pitched|inning pitched]].  ==see also== *[[major league baseball consecutive games played streaks]] *[[list of nhl players with 500 consecutive games played]]  ==references== {{reflist}} {{baseball-stub}} {{defaultsort:games played}} [[category:baseball statistics]]
