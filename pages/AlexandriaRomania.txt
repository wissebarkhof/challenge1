'''alexandria''' ({{ipa-ro|aleˈksandri.a|-|ro-alexandria.ogg}}) is the capital city of the [[teleorman county]], [[romania]]. it is located south-west of [[bucharest]], towards the bulgarian border. the city is situated on the [[vedea river]] and has 45,434 inhabitants.  {{historical populations |source = census data |1912 |15785 |1930 |19350 |1948 |17840 |1956 |19294 |1966 |21898 |1977 |37340
