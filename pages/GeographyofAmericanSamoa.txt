water]] |}  [[american samoa]], located within the geographical region of [[oceania]], is one of only two possessions of the [[united states]] in the [[southern hemisphere]], the other being [[jarvis island]]. its total land area is {{convert|76.8|sqmi|km2}}—slightly larger than [[washington, d.c.]]—consisting of five rugged, volcanic islands and two [[coral atoll]]s. the five volcanic islands are: [[tutuila]], [[aunu'u]], [[ofu-olosega|ofu]], [[ofu-olosega|olosega]], [[tau, american samoa|ta'u]]. the coral atolls are: [[swains island|swains]], and [[rose atoll]]. of the seven islands, rose atoll is an uninhabited marine national monument.  due to its positioning in the south pacific ocean, it is frequently hit by typhoons between december and march.<ref>{{cite web
